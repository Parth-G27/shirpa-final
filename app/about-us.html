The beginning

Since 1998, Shipra Enterprises has been at the forefront of the construction chemicals industry, consistently delivering innovative solutions and achieving remarkable milestones.

our journey and achievements

Shipra is the Leading Construction Chemicals Supplier in South Gujarat, from Baroda to Vapi.

We have revolutionized the way construction projects are undertaken and completed. Our journey has been defined by a rich history of achievements and a relentless pursuit of innovation.

Throughout the years, we have continuously expanded our product range, introducing cutting-edge formulations that have set new standards for quality, durability, and sustainability.

Challenges we overcame

As pioneers, the first challenge we faced was a lack of awareness and therefore lack of need and will for using construction chemicals. As a team, we worked hard to raise awareness and deliver customized and successful solutions for unique construction problems.We also successfully managed a typical challenge of malicious campaigning by business rivals with our trust in our products and services, technical and communication expertise, and our Service First approach.
