Schmitten Factory, Surat

Achieving Optimal Hygiene Standards at Schmitten

Challenge

Schmitten, a distinguished chocolate factory in Surat, marks a significant milestone in our commitment to excellence. Schmitten required an anti-bacterial environment that adheres to the stringent standards of the FDA. Recognizing the unique challenges of chocolate production and the paramount importance of maintaining impeccable hygiene, our team engaged in a collaborative effort.

Our Solutions

Our journey began by partnering with food consultants to gain in-depth insights into the stringent hygiene standards prevalent in the food industry, particularly those set by the FDA. This collaborative effort allowed us to tailor our solutions precisely to Schmitten's unique requirements.

Our team's expertise and dedication led us to propose a comprehensive solution for Schmitten. We recommended Polyurethane (PU) coating for the walls and ceilings, providing not only a protective layer but also incorporating anti-bacterial properties. This ensured a hygienic environment that surpassed the rigorous standards set by the FDA.

Addressing the crucial junctions where walls meet the floor, we introduced and executed PU concrete and Epoxy coving for flooring. This innovative solution not only enhanced the aesthetic appeal of the space but also acted as an effective barrier against bacteria, leaving no room for contamination.

Our understanding of construction chemicals and their applications, combined with a commitment to quality, played a pivotal role in the flawless execution of the anti-bacterial environment at Schmitten. Shipra not only comprehended the unique requirements of the project but also provided the right solutions.

This project shows the importance of the transformative impact construction chemicals can have when applied with precision and innovation. Beyond merely supplying chemicals, we believe in creating environments that foster excellence. The success achieved with the Schmitten project is a shining example of our dedication.
