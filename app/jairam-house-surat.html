Jainam House

Jainam House, Surat

Shipra's Innovative Solution For Long-Lasting Exterior Appeal of Jainam House

Challenge

Jainam Pvt Ltd, required that the Smart Exterior Concrete protection of their Office Building which would be exposed to harsh sun or weather conditions, needed to retain the appeal. The goal was not just durability but to maintain the building's original aesthetic charm over time.

Our Solutions

Shipra is armed with a strategic and innovative solution. Recognizing the need for both resilience and enduring allure, Shipra proposed an Ingenious Combination of water-repellent technology and advanced coating. This dynamic silicone-based coating was tailored to shield the building from the inevitable wear and tear associated with prolonged exposure to the elements.

Execution

Going above and beyond the proposed solution, Shipra ensured the successful implementation of the solution. The application team meticulously applied the recommended silicone-based water-repellent coating solution, overseeing the process from start to finish.

Results

The impact was transformative. The building not only stood resilient against the sun's harsh rays but retained its original open aesthetic. Shipra's innovative approach not only protected the structure from weathering but also preserved the distinctive look that Jainam sought.

Shipra's commitment to providing comprehensive solutions that go beyond conventional expectations. By addressing both durability and aesthetic concerns, Shipra's Innovative water repellent coating, coupled with on-site application expertise, delivered a result that not only met but exceeded Jainam's vision for a building that stands the test of time, both functionally and aesthetically.
